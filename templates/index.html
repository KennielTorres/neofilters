Why neofilters?

As known, Spotify offers a handful of options to filter your playlists to your likings. However, at times it feels like you want to be surgical about it. Here at neofilters, we are able to offer you advanced filtering options such as filtering by the release date, or even a range of dates, popularity, amongst others.

We want to give you the most control over your music, because we believe that nobody knows you better than yourself. We hope you enjoy this platform!
