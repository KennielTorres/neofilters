Why neofilters?

As known, Spotify offers a handful of options to filter and curate your playlists to your likings. However, at times it feels like there aren't enough options. Here at neofilters, we are able to offer you the traditional and even more advanced filtering options such as filtering by the release date, or even a range of dates, and popularity, amongst others.

We want to give you the most control over your music, because we believe that nobody knows you better than yourself. We hope you enjoy this platform!
